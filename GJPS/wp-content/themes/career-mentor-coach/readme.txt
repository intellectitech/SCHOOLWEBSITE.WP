== Description ==

Career Mentor Coach is a modern WordPress block theme for career mentors, executive coaches, interview advisors, and business-growth consultants. Built with Full Site Editing, it helps coaches present mentoring programs, offer free strategy calls, publish career resources, and guide clients through structured coaching journeys.

The front page is assembled from individual section patterns, and the header and footer are provided as global template parts for Full Site Editing.

Perfect for:
* Career Coaches & Mentors
* Executive and Leadership Coaches
* Interview and Resume Advisors
* Business Growth Consultants
* Online Mentoring Programs
* Personal Brand and LinkedIn Specialists

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
