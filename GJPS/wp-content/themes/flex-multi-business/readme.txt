== Description ==

Flex Multi Business is a modern and versatile theme designed for a range of sectors, including startups, consulting firms, corporations, digital agencies, finance companies, IT services, law firms, marketing agencies, construction companies, and small enterprises. It creates a professional online presence, making it perfect for building online stores, dropshipping setups, print-on-demand sites, and websites for various digital services. With its clean and stylish layout, Flex Multi Business includes essential features such as a customizable header with a call-to-action, an engaging slider to promote key services, an informative About Us section, and an interactive Services area. A dynamic Latest News & Blogs section enhances your content marketing strategy while ensuring the theme is fully responsive and mobile-friendly for seamless performance across desktops, tablets, and smartphones. Built for speed and efficiency, this theme integrates with WooCommerce for e-commerce functionality, enabling online payments and facilitating product and service sales. It also supports Contact Form 7 for lead generation and inquiries along with Yoast SEO for improved search visibility. With one-click demo import, SEO optimization, and cross-browser compatibility, Flex Multi Business is a lightweight, modern, and customizable solution for launching a sleek and conversion-focused business website effectively. Whether you’re involved in consulting, marketing, or operating within a corporate structure, Flex Multi Business positions your venture—be it a creative agency or a digital business—for success.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
